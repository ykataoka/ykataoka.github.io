Yasuyuki Kataoka


Research Scientist, NTT Research, Inc.

My research addresses hemodynamic control problems through an integrated framework that combines cardiovascular modeling, pharmacological and device-based modeling, and advanced control algorithm design. I conduct validation studies using both in-silico and in-vivo models, aiming to bridge methodological development and clinical application. My work is motivated by clinical translation, with a particular focus on acute heart failure. I am trained in control engineering and data science, with applications in biomedical systems.

Research Interests

  • Hemodynamic control and optimization
  • Cardiovascular modeling
  • Model-based and Data-driven Control Algorithm
  • Clinical decision support system
